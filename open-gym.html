Hanu Open Gym

Hanu Sports is a children’s activity center that caters to the age group 3 – 15 years. Activities of Children’s Open Gym help kids in enhancing focus and concentration using our traditional sports innovatively.

  • Children’s Open Gym is a physical activity center at Residential Doorstep with the mission of fitness and well-being. 
  • The gym brings together holistic activities on a dedicated platform where your child enjoys spending time and looks forward to attending.
  • Children’s Open Gym includes a 60 minutes activity arena at your residential location and engages your kid in various innovative and result-oriented health activities.
  • The daily physical activity at this age results in various health benefits associated with Hearth, Stomach, Bones, Brain, eyes, etc. A healthy kid can achieve his goals with focus and concentration.

Activities in the Open Gym:

  • Yogasana,Pranayam & Meditation: In today’s competitive world, children are leaving a very stressful life. Yoga is a low-cost, helpful tool that can have a positive impact on children with lots of health benefits.Meditation helps to enhance the focus, boost confidence, and build empathy and happiness in children.
  • Warm-Up : Surya Namaskar is an incredible get ready for kids, as it stretches the whole body and helps move each joint and significant muscle group. This smooth transition between the postures causes one concentrate can markedly affect the creativity and focus levels of the child.
  • Athletics And Drills : Athletics teach your children lifelong healthy habits. It includes group activities which increase alertness.
  • Gymnastics : Gymnastics includes exercises that increase strength, speed, power, coordination, flexibility, and balance.
  • Self-Defense : In today’s world, every child should how to defend themselves against physical attacks. Also in self-defense, kids learn discipline, dedication, perseverance, etc.
  • Story-Telling : The story is the best medium of explanation.
  • Spirited Songs : It creates an integral sense among kids.
  • Geeta Shlok : Bhagwad Geeta is nothing but the FAQ(frequently asked questions) which includes all the questions and answers about life. If children learn Geeta-shlok at an early age then it definitely changes their approach towards life.
    It creates qualities like forgiveness, tolerance, calm, welfare of the world, etc.
